Comet

Comet is a game where you crash land on a toxic wasteland after responding to a nearby distress call and are almost obliterated. Stranded, and struggling to survive with your up to 3 teammates, you scramble together resources to repair your spaceship and attempt to return back to somewhere safe to repair your ship fully, whilst avoiding enemy ships and other hazards.

Will you survive the perilous journey to return back home? Or will you buckle under pressure and crash-land, ending your journey? Find out in early 2024 when Comet releases!
